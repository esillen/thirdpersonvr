Download the official tag image from the AprilTag project, then print it at 100 percent scale on matte paper.

How to mount it:

  1. Print on matte white paper at 100 percent scale.
  2. Mount flat on stiff card or foam board.
  3. Attach it to the top of the headset, cap, or headband.
  4. Keep the full square visible and avoid glare.
